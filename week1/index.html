Master Mobile UX Notes

 How to rewire your brain for mobile first UX

  • Things to remember
  • Desktop computer sales have been declining since 2011
  • Users are seen using mobile devices most often
  • Start small and scale up
  • Easier, cheaper, more effective
  • Mobile is not a fad

How to improve mobile UX by understanding users

  • Be there - immediate
  • Be useful - relevant give users what they want fast)
  • Be quick - frictionless (reduce cognitive load)
  • Reachability
  • How do users hold their phones?
  • Make things easy for users to reach
  • Speed matters
  • .5 sec delay = 20% drop in traffic on Google
  • Remember not all users have high speed access
  • Ensure quick loading
  • Reduce image usage
  • Use SVG and Web fonts
  • Optimize and minify CSS and JS
  • GZIP files to eliminate extra data

Designing intuitive and mobile friendly forms

  • Keep number of fields down
  • Make fields larger for tapping
  • Make use of float labels
  • If a long form is necessary - paginate and use a progress indicator
  • Use auto complete
  • Show passwords but offer a method for masking
  • Provide visual indicators
  • Auto advance for numbers like SSN and card numbers
  • Offer appropriate keyboards
  • Don't force copy and paste with phone numbers - use a link to allow users to tap and call

Designing a better mobile advertising UX

  • Before you advertise - research
  • Ads and content small and simple
  • Make use of small video ads
  • Full size ad under content viewable through gap in scrol
  • lStop using pop-ups and pop-overs
  • Keep advertising simple and sleek
  • Content first keep - advertising unobtrusive
